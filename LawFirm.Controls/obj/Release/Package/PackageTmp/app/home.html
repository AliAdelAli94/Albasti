About Us

The Law Offices of Al Basti Advocates & Legal Consultants is a fully serviced duly licensed leading Dubai law firm based in the UAE. Our Lawyers have expertise to deal with the diverse and complex needs of clients seeking legal advice or planning to establish, structure or restructure their business, ventures or projects in Dubai and UAE.
